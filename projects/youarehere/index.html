You Are Here

An ambient art piece that slowly evolves over time. A reflection on loss, change, and the passage of time.

Visit the page now and come back in a day, a month, or (just under/over) a year to see how it's changed. The piece is inspired by planetary orbits and the cyclical nature of life. Even if you had a time machine, you couldn't go back to the same place when even the solar system itself is moving.
