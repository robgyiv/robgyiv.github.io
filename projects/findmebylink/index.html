FindMeByLink

findmeby.link helps people answer a simple, high-intent question fast: who’s coming to my city, and when? It turns travel schedules into something you can actually explore — especially for tattoo artists, tattoo studios, musicians, and music venues.

The experience is built around two modes that work together:

  • Search by city, date, and profile type when you know what you want.
  • Flip to the map when you want to browse what’s nearby.
  • Clustering + auto-fit bounds keep the map usable even when there’s a lot going on.
